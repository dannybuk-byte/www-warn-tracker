💬 Section 4

Communications & Outreach

The 60-day WARN window is the critical outreach period. Workers have been notified but haven't left yet.

🕒 The Window Day 1–15: Worker receives notice. Day 15–45: Prime outreach window — email works, no NDA. Day 45–60: Final days, NDA risk. Day 60+: Work email dead. You must reach them in the first 45 days.
